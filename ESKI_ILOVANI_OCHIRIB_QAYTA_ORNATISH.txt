PHARM360° NOMI VA IKONKASINI YANGILASH

MUHIM: GitHub/Render yangilangani bilan oldin o‘rnatilgan “Marjon PRO” ilovasi avtomatik nomini almashtirmasligi mumkin.

TELEFONDA:
1. “Marjon PRO” ustiga uzoq bosing.
2. “O‘chirish / Удалить” ni bosing.
3. Chrome sozlamasidan sayt ma’lumotlari va keshini tozalang.
4. https://marjon-platform.onrender.com/?source=pharm360-pwa-v14 manzilini oching.
5. Chrome menyusi (⋮) → “Ilovani o‘rnatish / Установить приложение”.
6. Endi nom “Pharm360°”, ikonka yangi yashil-ko‘k logo bo‘ladi.

KOMPYUTERDA:
1. Ish stolidagi eski “Marjon” ikonkasini o‘chiring.
2. Chrome’da saytni ?source=pharm360-pwa-v14 bilan oching.
3. Brauzer keshini tozalang va Ctrl+F5 bosing.
4. Chrome menyusi → “Pharm360° ni o‘rnatish” yoki yangi yorliq yarating.
